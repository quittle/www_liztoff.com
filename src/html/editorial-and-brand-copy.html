Editorial & Brand Copy

  • AXEL ARIGATO

    Axel Arigato was launched with one core goal: to embrace the now while always searching for the tomorrow. Founders Max Svärda and Albin Johansson made their mark on the industry by questioning its entire structure and reinventing it to deliver couture-quality footwear at an affordable price tag. Expanding to include accessories and streetwear staples, the brand forgoes the typical seasonal collections and traditional sales channels in lieu of engaging with customers directly and releasing a new style every week. The Axel Arigato world is made up of references to music, art and architecture illustrated and channelled through its own unique, disruptive and counter-cultural voice. The result is easy-to-wear designs that are graphic, editorial and subtle.

  • PURFUME

    Finding the perfect luxury perfume is not just an investment in the lavish experience of the present but is about a lifetime of impressions. As the strongest tie to memory, scent can instantly transport you back to a specific moment – a thrilling professional success, parent’s embrace or even your wedding day. Curate your own olfactory journey with the many forms of fragrance, from eau de parfums to body sprays.

  • WOMENS BURBERRY ACCESSORIES

    Burberry women’s accessories allow you to access the luxury heritage, style and craftsmanship of the British brand with every ensemble. Under the creative direction of Riccardo Tisci, these touches have been elevated from the archive to new heights of desirability. Accent trench coats with iconic check scarves, cinch dresses and denim with monogram belts and sign-off essentials with keychains and phone cases that showcase your brand allegiance.

  • FLORAL STREET

    Floral Street is a modern luxury fragrance brand devoted to thoughtful practices; all of its perfumes are vegan, cruelty-free, crafted from sustainably sourced ingredients and presented in eco-friendly packaging. Born from the streets of London, it is British in every aspect of its being, from its scent inspirations to the Covent Garden flagship store. Coordinate its long-lasting eau de parfums with each day’s mood, as you would an outfit.

  • JO MALONE LONDON

    Jo Malone London weaves stories of British landscapes in perfumery. From the dramatic cliffs of Dover to the picturesque Cotswolds, its fragrances demonstrate a singular dedication to the label’s heritage and the endless fount of inspiration that it provides. English Pear & Freesia, for example, mirrors the golden spell that falls over the countryside in autumn, while the Amber & Lavender cologne evokes the cosmopolitan gardens of the capital.

  • WOMENS GUCCI SCARVES

    Tied around the neck, garnishing a handbag or securing a loose ponytail, Gucci scarves have become a necessity for those who wish to count themselves among the fashion elite thanks to the label’s covetable House codes. The GG Supreme print, found gracing both wool and silk scarves, is among the most sought-after designs of the brand, while reimaginings of the Web stripe and pops of florals delight season after season.

  • CHRISTMAS TREE TOPPERS

    Christmas trees just aren’t complete until they’ve been crowned with a tree topper. The tradition captured merrymakers’ imaginations in the mid-1800s when an illustration of Queen Victoria, Prince Albert and the royal family gathered around their angel-topped Christmas tree appeared in the Illustrated London News. Angel and star designs have since been favoured for their connection to the Nativity, while glass renditions are perfect for finishing off contemporary firs.

  • PERUS

    PerUs crafts exceptional, truly singular wines for those who desire to lead a remarkable and inspired life. Head Winemaker Russell Bevan nurtures each vintage from fruit to bottle, exemplifying the excellence of the label’s three Californian vineyards: Tench, Sugarloaf Mountain and Edcora. The unique elevations, climates and soils of each works a near-alchemical magic upon their native grapes to create wines marked by intense flavours, sumptuous textures and fully realised personalities. It is in this way that the collective of successful entrepreneurs and gifted professionals at PerUs translates its central philosophy: an enriched life is measured not in possessions, but in the vibrant experiences shared with friends and family.

  • CHRISTMAS BISCUITS

    The most wonderful time of the year wouldn’t be complete without Christmas biscuits. The classic, warm flavours of gingerbread, fruit-studded Florentine biscuits − everyone has their festive favourite, but one thing is for sure: enjoying the delectable mainstays is a centuries-old tradition that never fails to ignite the childlike wonder of the season in all of us, especially when they hail from our legendary Food Halls.

  • PORT

    Luxury port represents the pinnacle of fortified wines. From the mellow tawny to the bright, nutty white and sublimely complex vintage, the rolling richness of port wine is equally suited to social tipples as it is to intimate fireside sipping. Enjoy it as a multi-layered cocktail ingredient, paired with nut and cheese courses, as a dessert all on its own or as the perfect nightcap alongside fine cigars.

  • MENS GUCCI CARDIGANS

    Gucci’s creative director Alessandro Michele has been instrumental in defining the scope of modern menswear, and one of his most notable, season-spanning contributions is the resurrection of the men’s cardigan. No longer is it relegated to the realm of stuffy academia; the ultra-chic Gucci cardigan comes in many forms – from pared-back wool designs to pattern-forward statement pieces – and slots seamlessly into your rotation thanks to the moment’s predilection for layering.

  • ROSÉ WINE

    See the world through rosé-coloured glasses with our thoughtfully curated collection. Brighter and more refreshing than most reds with the fruity acidity of whites, rosé wines are equally suited to social sipping at garden parties and intimate full-course meals thanks to their sparsity of tannins. Enjoy dry rosé wines alongside lighter dishes like fish, grilled chicken and salads or choose a sweeter vintage to tame spice and balance smokey flavours.
